About Me

Hello World! I am a Musician, Educator, Restauranteur and aspiring web developer currently residing in Los Angeles, California. I studied Music Education over the course of 5 years at California State University, Los Angeles and currently hold a Bachelor's Degree in Music Education. I was the Music Director at Fair Oaks Academy in Altadena for over 4 years, with an emphasis on Instrumental Music and Jazz. My experience in teaching and performing has taken me around the country and the world. When I am not performing/teaching, I am also the General Manager of the Chipotle Mexican Grill at the Glendale Galleria.

My aspirations for web developing began when I was a teenager, connecting with my friends on social media on blogs such as "Xanga" and "MySpace". I loved manipulating HTML code and found that editing specific code would render a whole world of possibilities in designing my page.

Although life has taken me through many phases, nothing excites me more than the possibility of becoming a future web developer in the industry. Here on my porfolio you will find links to some of my proud work in HTML/CSS/JavaScript. Please feel free to reach out and connect with me!
